Privacy Policy for Face Analyzer AI

Effective date: March 23, 2026

Face Symmetry Analyzer (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and the choices available to you when using the App.

1. Information We Access

Camera access

The App uses your device camera so you can scan your face and generate facial symmetry and facial analysis results.

Face images

Images used for analysis are processed on-device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Analysis results and Result history

If you use save or history features, your analysis results and result history are stored locally on your device.

Purchase information

If you purchase Face Analyzer AI PRO, billing and payment processing are handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Diagnostics and analytics

We currently do not use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, or other third-party analytics SDKs.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • provide facial symmetry and facial analysis features;
  • show your scan results inside the App;
  • store saved results and result history on your device;
  • enable sharing features that you choose to use;
  • manage PRO feature access through Google Play billing;
  • maintain the performance, security, and reliability of the App.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your information except in the following cases:

  • with service providers required to operate the App, such as Google Play billing, where necessary;
  • when you explicitly use your device’s sharing features to share content;
  • when required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Face images are not retained after processing.
  • Saved results and result history remain available until you delete them, uninstall the App, or clear the App’s data from your device.
  • If you contact us with a deletion or privacy request, we will respond at: support.beyond.theai.labs@gmail.com

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Face Analyzer AI is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services necessary for operation, such as Google Play billing. These services may process limited information as required to provide their services under their own privacy policies.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to use camera-based features, but some functionality of the App may not work without camera access.

You may also remove saved results at any time by deleting App data, uninstalling the App, or using available in-app controls if provided.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted at the same Privacy Policy URL with a revised effective date.
